About

A focused builder for complex, high-stakes ideas.

Founder and systems builder exploring lunar infrastructure, robotics, and high-integrity engineering.

My work is driven by first-principles thinking, disciplined research, and the belief that early technical clarity creates better companies. I like turning uncertain, complex ideas into readable systems, test plans, models, and narratives that people can act on.

I am especially interested in space infrastructure, robotics, lunar regolith mechanics, autonomous construction, and the founder craft required to move a hard technical project from concept toward evidence.
