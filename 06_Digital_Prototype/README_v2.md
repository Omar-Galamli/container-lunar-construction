## v2 Architecture Target

The v2 model represents:

- 8 m x 6 m x 4 m open-top rectangular container body.
- Raised internal floor and protected underfloor equipment deck.
- Rear service hatch.
- Front sliding-lift toothed bucket.
- Bucket lateral slide, vertical lift, and curl/dump motion.
- Four identical long-reach robotic arms on side rails.
- Two arms per side, with rail-carriage travel along the 8 m body length.
- Arm docking/coupling behavior for cooperative handling.
- Distributed camera/sensor coverage.
- Body shield panels with functional openings.
- No gantry, no helical anchors, no stabilizers, no outriggers, and no closed top.

## Status

The v2 Blender and STL files are concept/prototype artifacts, not fabrication-release drawings. They still require detailed structural design, load cases, actuator sizing, material selection, tolerance review, and physical testing.
